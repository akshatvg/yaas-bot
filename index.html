About YAAS- Bot

What is YAAS- Bot?

YAAS- Bot is a bot to repost popular posts from VIT Club & Chapters acting as a one stop platform keeping everyone updated about happenings in VIT University.

Advantages of following our page:

  • Know all active and exisiting club & chapters.
  • Follow just one page to view posts for all domains.
  • Know similar events as yours you might've missed.
  • Extra publicity for clubs & chapters.
